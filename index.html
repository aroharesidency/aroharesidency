Our Story

Aroha Means Love,
In Every Detail

Aroha means love — and that's exactly what this place is to us.

Every detail at Aroha Residency is built around that feeling. From the warmth when you arrive to the comfort in every room, everything is designed to feel personal.

Located in a peaceful hillside just 20 minutes from Manali town, Aroha offers the best of both worlds — close to everything, yet far enough to truly relax.

With 13 well-designed rooms across Standard, Deluxe, and Family Suites — all featuring private balconies and stunning mountain and garden views — there's a perfect space for every traveler.

The property also includes an in-house café, lush gardens, apple orchards, and private parking.

A stay at Aroha isn't just relaxing — it's something you'll want to come back to.
